Child Tax Credit 2024: Amount, Eligibility & Refundable Portion

📅 Updated 2024⏱ 5 min read

The Child Tax Credit (CTC) is $2,000 per qualifying child under age 17 in 2024. Up to $1,700 of the credit is refundable as the Additional Child Tax Credit (ACTC)—meaning you can receive it even if you owe no tax. The credit phases out for higher-income families.

Qualifying Child Requirements

To claim the Child Tax Credit, each child must meet all these tests:

  • Age: Under 17 at the end of the tax year
  • Relationship: Your son, daughter, stepchild, foster child, sibling, or descendant of any of these
  • Dependent: Claimed as a dependent on your return
  • Residency: Lived with you for more than half the year
  • Support: Did not provide more than half of their own support
  • Citizenship: U.S. citizen, national, or resident alien
  • SSN: Must have a valid Social Security Number issued before the tax return due date

2024 Income Phase-Out

The CTC phases out by $50 for every $1,000 of income above the threshold:

Filing StatusPhase-Out BeginsFull Credit Eliminated At (1 child)
Married Filing Jointly$400,000$440,000
Single / HoH / MFS$200,000$240,000

The Refundable Portion (ACTC)

If your CTC exceeds your tax liability, you may claim the Additional Child Tax Credit (ACTC) as a refund—up to $1,700 per child in 2024. To qualify for the ACTC:

  • You must have earned income of at least $2,500
  • The ACTC equals 15% of earned income above $2,500 (up to the $1,700 cap per child)
  • Example: $30,000 earned income — ($30,000 − $2,500) × 15% = $4,125 max ACTC (capped at $1,700 per child)

Credit for Other Dependents

If your dependent doesn't qualify for the CTC (age 17+, or no SSN), you may claim the Credit for Other Dependents: a $500 nonrefundable credit per qualifying dependent. Same income phase-outs apply.

💡
Claim via Schedule 8812

Both the CTC and ACTC are calculated on Schedule 8812 (Credits for Qualifying Children and Other Dependents), attached to your Form 1040. Tax software handles this automatically when you enter each dependent's information.
